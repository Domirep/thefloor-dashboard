How it works

Buy a seat with $FLOOR. Cheap early, pricier as the session fills. Every seat you hold earns commissions from every seat bought after you.
Each buy resets the bell and makes you the one holding the floor.
When the bell rings, the last buyer wins. Anti-snipe: a buy in the final 2 minutes pushes the bell back.
The prize is operators, not tokens. The pot buys the highest operator(s) it can afford and sends the NFT(s) to the winner. Leftover burns into a firm.
