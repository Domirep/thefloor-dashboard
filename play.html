The Floor · desks & operators

An idle strategy game: your desk holds operators, operators produce alpha, and your alpha is your share of the game's $FLOOR emissions.

  1. Get a wallet on Robinhood Chain with a little ETH.
  2. On the official site, connect and create a desk (0.01 ETH). The recruiter shown at creation is written on-chain and can never change — it's shown before you sign.
  3. Recruit the free starter operator. It starts producing alpha immediately.
  4. Collect PnL when it's worth the gas. Spend $FLOOR on stronger operators (75% of in-game spend burns) and upgrade the desk when you run out of seats or bandwidth.
  5. Emissions halve roughly monthly, so early compounding counts. Check your numbers on the dashboard's My Desk tab, or point an agent at /mcp and ask for get_strategy.

StonkBrokers · broker NFTs

4,444 pixel brokers. Each one owns a real on-chain wallet (ERC-6551) seeded with a tokenized stock, and can earn stock dividends from the protocol's trading fees.

  1. The mint is over — brokers trade on the official Anvil AMM marketplace, paid in $STONKBROKER.
  2. Before buying one, check its wallet on the broker inspector: contents can be moved out by the seller until your purchase lands, and a paid activation resets on every transfer.
  3. Activate a tier (paid in $STONKBROKER, 50% burns) to turn on dividend drops — they land every ~10 minutes, weighted by tier (tier 5 earns 3.33× tier 1).
  4. Dividends accrue inside the broker's wallet. Track any broker, and the leaderboard, on /brokers.

One wallet, both games

A broker's wallet is a full account — it can hold any token or NFT and use any app on this chain, and whatever it holds travels with the broker when it changes hands. That includes a Floor desk: positions on The Floor normally can't be sold or moved at all, but a desk inside a broker's wallet goes wherever the NFT goes.

What a desk adds to a broker (capabilities, not price promises):
· a second income stream — FLOOR emissions alongside the stock dividend drops
· the desk survives transfers, so it's the one Floor position a future owner actually receives
· earnings accrue inside the NFT instead of scattering across your wallets
· anyone — human or agent — can verify the desk on-chain before buying the broker
  1. Add the agent endpoint to Claude, Claude Code, or Cursor: /mcp. This flow is MCP-only by design — no website here builds or submits these transactions.
  2. Ask it to prepare_broker_floor_desk for your broker's id. The tool verifies everything on-chain first — that the broker is activated (a desk is a perk of activated brokers; activate first if it isn't), that you own it, that its wallet has no desk, the exact cost, the referrer (changeable) — and refuses with an error if any check fails. Only a fully verified request gets a transaction back.
  3. Sign in your own wallet as the broker's owner. The 0.01 ETH rides along with your signature; the broker's wallet needs no prior funding.
  4. The desk now belongs to the broker's wallet. Collect its earnings the same way (prepare_broker_floor_collect, same verification) — the $FLOOR lands in the broker's wallet.
Three facts to keep straight before you rely on any of this:
· a Floor desk lives on the broker's wallet and survives transfers
· a paid activation resets on every transfer — activate after moving a broker, not before
· everything else in the wallet is removable by the owner until a sale lands — verify contents at sale time

There is deliberately no "connect wallet" button on this site. Transactions come out of the MCP unsigned, only after every verification passes, and you sign them in your own wallet — this server never holds keys and never broadcasts. Nobody's broker wallet has opened a desk yet.
