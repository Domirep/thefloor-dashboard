Inspect a broker

Each broker has its own on-chain wallet (ERC-6551). Enter an id to see what's inside: the stock it was seeded with, dividends received, activation tier, and the art — read live from the chain.

The wallet is a full on-chain account, not just a vault: it can hold any token or NFT and use any app on Robinhood Chain, and whatever it accumulates travels with the broker when it changes hands. One example: it can hold a desk on The Floor — how to play both games. Agent tools are at /mcp.

If you're buying a broker: any Floor desk lives on the broker's wallet and survives the sale. A paid activation does not — the contract clears it on every transfer, so you'll re-activate. Anything else in the wallet — stocks, tokens, operator NFTs, ETH — can be moved out by the seller before your purchase lands, so check the wallet at sale time. Not an appraisal, not financial advice.

Activation tiers

—

Activating a broker (paid in $STONKBROKER, 50% burned) turns on its dividend drops. Weight = share multiplier per drop: tier 5 earns 3.33× tier 1.

Stock dividends

—

The StockBooster swaps accumulated fees into tokenized stocks and drops them into activated brokers' wallets, weighted by tier. ETH-in is the value entering each round; round times are interpolated from block numbers (approx).
