Weekly Firm Championship

top 3 split the pool · closes Fri 23:59 ET
How it works. Each firm's score is its members' trading profit on Robinhood Chain, added up, Monday–Friday (ET). The top 3 firms split the prize pool 65 / 25 / 10, paid Saturday. The pool grows all week as the game is played.
Worth knowing: the rules say realized profit (“close a trade to book it”), but the scores we track move with live prices — a firm's rank shifts even when it isn't trading, so a lead isn't locked until Friday's close.

FAQ & Wiki

how the game works · quick answers
Getting started
What is The Floor?
An on-chain idle-strategy game on Robinhood Chain. You run a trading desk, seat pixel-art operator NFTs on it, and they generate $FLOOR for you around the clock — even while you're offline.
How do I start playing?
Head to thefloor.sh, connect a wallet, and claim your free Kitchen Table desk and starter operator. From there you reinvest earnings (or buy $FLOOR) to upgrade your desk and recruit stronger operators.
Does it cost anything to try?
No — the Kitchen Table desk and starter operator are free. Spending only starts when you upgrade your desk or recruit more operators, and that spend is priced in $FLOOR.
Desks · Operators · Alpha
What does my desk do?
Your desk sets your capacity: how many seats you have and how much bandwidth your operators can use. There are 11 tiers, from Kitchen Table to the Bell Room. Higher tiers also grant permanent prestige — a multiplier on your total alpha (up to +80%). The full table is in the Desks section.
What is alpha?
Alpha is your earning power. Every operator contributes alpha; your share of the network's total alpha is your share of the daily $FLOOR emissions. Double your alpha and you double your earnings (until others grow theirs too).
What is bandwidth?
Each operator consumes bandwidth, and your desk only has so much. Stronger operators bring more alpha but eat more bandwidth — the calculator lets you plan the best roster that fits your desk before you spend.
Which operator should I buy?
Check the Operators table — it shows live $FLOOR/day and payback days for each one at current emission rates. As a rule of thumb, the best value is the highest alpha-per-FLOOR that still fits your bandwidth.
Earning · Emissions · Halving
How much can I earn?
Emissions are split pro-rata by alpha. The live rate per 1 alpha (in $FLOOR and USD) is in State of the Floor — multiply by your total alpha for your daily rate, or just paste your address into My Report for exact numbers from the chain.
What is the halving?
The emission rate halves every 30-day epoch, exactly like Bitcoin's schedule but faster. The countdown in the top bar reads the exact halving timestamp from the game contract. Earlier players earn at richer rates — that's the incentive to start now.
Do I need to claim my earnings?
Yes — earnings accrue as pending PnL inside the game until you claim, which mints them to your wallet. Unclaimed PnL isn't lost; it just sits there (the dashboard tracks the network-wide total in State of the Floor).
$FLOOR token & economy
What's the total supply?
Hard cap 17.92M $FLOOR, of which 95% (17.024M) is the emissions pool paid out to players over time. Only a small fraction has been emitted so far — the progress bar is in State of the Floor.
What happens to $FLOOR I spend in-game?
75% of every FLOOR spent on upgrades and recruits is burned forever, permanently reducing supply — and founding Firms burns even more. The deflation is real: more $FLOOR has already been burned than currently exists (live total in State of the Floor, read from the token contract's burn ledger).
Why does the market cap here differ from other sites?
We compute it as live price × true circulating supply — burned tokens and tokens parked at the dead address are excluded, read straight from the token contract. Some aggregators count those dead tokens and overstate the cap.
Where can I buy or sell $FLOOR?
Trading happens on Uniswap V3 on Robinhood Chain — easiest via the official site. $FLOOR is an experimental game token — never risk more than you can afford to lose.
Firms & boosts
Should I join a Firm?
Usually yes — it's a free alpha boost. Members pool FLOOR to level a shared office that boosts everyone's alpha (+2% Startup up to +22% Penthouse). Founding your own costs 2,500 FLOOR and unlocks the Principal (+5%) soulbound boost.
What are soulbound boosts?
Permanent, non-transferable multipliers on your alpha: The Intern (+10%), The Principal (+5%, firm founders), and X Badge (+2%, connect your X account). All boosts combined are capped at +50%. The calculator models them.
What are Deals?
Limited-time offers on the official site (discounted operators, bundles). When deals go live, the 🔔 indicator in the top bar of this dashboard lights up.
About this dashboard
Where does the data come from?
Everything is read live: game state straight from the game contract via Robinhood Chain RPC (desk, operators, alpha, pending PnL, burns, halving time), prices and volume from DEX data, holder lists from the block explorer. No manual numbers.
Is it safe to paste my address?
Yes — it's a read-only lookup of public on-chain data. This site never asks you to connect a wallet, sign anything, or share a private key. Never share your seed phrase with any site.
Is this the official site?
No — this is an unofficial fan-built dashboard. The official game is at thefloor.sh, and their docs are the canonical rules.
